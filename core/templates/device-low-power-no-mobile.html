Just a friendly reminder to recharge your mytaptrack clicker today!

To charge the device follow these steps:

  • Remove the back of the device.
  • REMOVE THE BATTERY from the mytaprack clicker.
  • Use a micro-usb cable to connect the battery to a power source.
  • After charging, replace the battery and the back of the device.
